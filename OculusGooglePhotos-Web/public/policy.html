Privacy Policy and Terms of Service

When you sign in to VR Gallery for Google Photos, you allow us to access your Google Photos library, along with other data received from the Google Photos API.

VR Gallery for Google Photos does not store any of your Google Photos data on our servers. All photos data is handled on-device.

VR Gallery for Google Photos' use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
